Saozimian

Saozimian is a type of Chinese noodle known for its unique texture and rich flavor.

This traditional noodle is a staple in many regional cuisines and is cherished for its versatility and nutritional value.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
    • Add the Saozimian noodles and cook according to the package instructions, usually about 5-7 minutes until they are al dente.
    • Drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add the minced garlic, ginger, and chopped green onions. Stir-fry for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    • Add the ground pork or beef. Cook until browned and crumbly, about 5-7 minutes.
    • Stir in the chili bean paste and fermented black beans (if using). Cook for another 2 minutes.
    • Add the soy sauce, dark soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and sugar. Mix well.
    • Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Combine and Serve:
    • Toss the cooked Saozimian noodles with the sauce until well coated.
    • Serve the noodles hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and chopped peanuts if desired.
